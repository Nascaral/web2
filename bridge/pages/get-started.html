Questions

Everything you need to know.

Is Bridge really free?

Yes, 100% free. No hidden fees, no premium tiers, no paywalls. We believe everyone deserves access to powerful financial tools, not just the wealthy.

How does Bridge make money?

We earn referral fees when you discover and sign up for financial products through our marketplace—but only if you choose to. Your data is never sold.

Is it safe to connect my bank accounts?

Absolutely. We use Plaid, the same service trusted by Venmo, Coinbase, and thousands of financial apps. We never see or store your bank login credentials.

Which banks does Bridge support?

Bridge supports over 12,000 financial institutions in the US, including all major banks, credit unions, investment accounts, and credit cards.

Can I delete my data?

Yes, at any time. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account and all your data will be permanently removed from our servers.

Do I need to switch banks?

No. Bridge works with your existing accounts. We simply connect to them securely so you can see everything in one place.
