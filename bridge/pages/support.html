Frequently Asked Questions

Is Bridge safe to use with my bank accounts?

Absolutely. Bridge uses bank-grade 256-bit encryption to protect your data both in transit and at rest. We never store your bank login credentials—all authentication is handled securely through our trusted partner, Plaid. Every Bridge login is also protected with multi-factor authentication.

How do I connect my bank accounts?

After downloading Bridge and creating your account, tap "Add Account" and search for your bank. You'll be guided through a secure login process powered by Plaid. Once connected, your accounts will sync automatically.

Why isn't my account balance updating?

Most accounts sync in real-time, but some banks may have a slight delay. Try refreshing your accounts by pulling down on the main screen. If the issue persists, try reconnecting your account or contact our support team.

Is Bridge really free?

Yes, Bridge is completely free to use. There are no hidden fees, no premium tiers, and no paywalls. We believe everyone deserves access to powerful financial tools.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account at any time from Settings > Account > Delete Account. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers. If you need assistance, contact our support team.
