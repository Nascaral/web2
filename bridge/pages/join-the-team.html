Shape the future
of personal finance.

At Bridge, we're building a financial platform that fixes a major frustration for everyday people: fragmented accounts, hidden fees, and confusing financial data. What takes traditional apps days, Bridge delivers in seconds. By aggregating accounts from the ground up, we replace scattered dashboards with software and turn static, outdated numbers into a live, contextual view of your finances.

Our mission: enable every financial decision to be made with instant and complete clarity. To achieve this, we're not just building a product; we're building a brand that will reshape how the world thinks about personal finance.

About The Team

We are a tight-knit team working virtually all over California

Our team has spent our careers at the intersection of finance and technology. Between all of us, we've built accounting systems at major fintech companies, led product at consumer finance startups, built previous companies from the ground up, and built finance teams from one to dozens. We've been founders, first finance hires, product leads, and founding engineers. We've led 0-to-1 launches and scaled systems to support millions in transactions. We understand our customers because we've been in their shoes, and care deeply about the future of finance that we're building.
